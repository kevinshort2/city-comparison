Chattanooga, TN

50+ trailheads including Cumberland Trail. Stone Fort world-class bouldering and Tennessee Wall. TN River Blueway, Chickamauga Lake, and the Ocoee Olympic venue for water sports. Enterprise South MTB and TN Riverwalk 13+ miles for cycling.

Greenville, SC

Blue Ridge foothills with Paris Mountain, Jones Gap, and the 77-mile Foothills Trail. Swamp Rabbit Trail spans 28 miles. Lakes Jocassee, Keowee, and Hartwell for water recreation. Multiple waterfalls within 30-60 minutes.

Columbia, SC

Congaree National Park and Harbison State Forest for hiking. Lake Murray covers 50K acres. Three Rivers Greenway for kayaking and canoeing. Riverbanks Zoo and Garden is top-rated nationally.

Lexington, KY (Baseline)

Raven Run, Legacy Trail, and Veterans Park for local hiking. Red River Gorge one hour east offers world-class climbing. Legacy Trail provides 12 miles of paved cycling. Kentucky River and Jacobson Park for fishing.

Coeur d'Alene, ID

Lake CDA stretches 25 miles. Trail of the Coeur d'Alenes runs 72 miles. Silver Mountain 35mi and Schweitzer 80mi for skiing. Premier state for fishing and hunting with lake, river, elk, deer, and bear opportunities.

Sarasota, FL

Siesta Key ranked #1 beach in the US. World-class Gulf fishing both inshore and offshore. Myakka River State Park covers 17K+ acres. Legacy Trail runs 20+ miles for cycling.

Bend, OR

223+ trails in Deschutes National Forest, Smith Rock, and Three Sisters Wilderness. Mt. Bachelor ski resort 22 miles away with 3,365 ft vertical. 300+ miles of singletrack including Phil's Trail. Premier trout fishing on the Deschutes River and Cascade Lakes.

Sisters, OR

Peterson Ridge trails, South Sister summit, Three Sisters Wilderness, and the Pacific Crest Trail. Hoodoo ski area 22mi, Mt. Bachelor 50-60 min. Metolius River for world-class fly fishing. Peterson Ridge and McKenzie Pass for mountain biking.

Flagstaff, AZ

Coconino National Forest and San Francisco Peaks (12,633 ft). 56-mile FUTS urban trail system. Grand Canyon 80mi and Sedona 30mi. Arizona Snowbowl: 777 acres, 8 lifts, 2,300 ft vertical. Outstanding mountain biking in Coconino NF. Mormon Lake and Upper/Lower Lake Mary for fishing.

Key Takeaways

Best Climate
Sarasota

251 sunny days per year with mild winters (72/52). No snow. The trade-off is hot, humid summers (91/75) and hurricane season.

Best Four-Season Climate
Greenville

220 sunny days with mild winters and moderate summers. Blue Ridge foothills provide cooler temperatures than the Carolina lowlands.

Best Outdoor Recreation
Bend / Sisters

Unmatched depth across hiking, skiing, mountain biking, fishing, and climbing. Central Oregon is an outdoor recreation destination, not just a place with good access.

Safest Overall
Coeur d'Alene / Columbia

Coeur d'Alene has very low crime (141 violent, 956 property per 100K). Columbia also ranks well on violent crime. Sisters is below national averages (150 violent, 1,532 property per 100K) but no longer the standout.

Best Equestrian Access
Lexington / Sisters

Lexington is the Horse Capital of the World with Kentucky Horse Park and unmatched equine culture. Sisters offers Cascade Meadow Ranch and endless national forest trails.

Best Commute / Walkability
Sarasota

Highest walk score (52) among all cities. Columbia and Bend tie for shortest average commute at 15 minutes.
